Welcome to Taj Palace, New Delhi

At the heart of India's capital, Taj Palace offers you the ultimate luxury experience with modern amenities and top-tier service. Whether you’re here for business or pleasure, our hotel provides an extraordinary blend of elegance and comfort.

Our spacious rooms, world-class dining, and serene surroundings make Taj Palace a preferred choice for travelers across the globe. Enjoy fine dining at our in-house restaurants, or rejuvenate yourself at our full-service spa. We ensure that every guest experiences the best of what New Delhi has to offer.

Rooms & Suites

Our hotel offers a variety of luxurious rooms and suites designed to provide maximum comfort and style. Whether you’re here for business or a special occasion, we have the perfect accommodation for you.

  • Standard Room: A cozy and comfortable space with modern decor and essential amenities, perfect for solo travelers or couples.
  • Deluxe Room: Larger rooms with beautiful city views and additional features like a king-size bed, flat-screen TV, and minibar.
  • Executive Suite: Spacious suites with a living area, work desk, and luxurious bath amenities for a more indulgent stay.
  • Presidential Suite: The epitome of luxury, our Presidential Suite offers a private living space, dining area, and dedicated butler service.
